About the MenstruaCie

The MenstruaCie keeps the Huygens building stocked with free menstrual products. They've placed boxes across the building and top them up on a rotating schedule.

The catch: nobody can be everywhere at once. A box that ran out yesterday may not be on the next round unless someone flags it. That's where you come in — a quick notification means the committee can prioritise wisely instead of guessing, and the next person who needs that box actually finds it stocked.

Same goes for damage: a broken lid, missing labels, anything that makes a box unusable. Drop a comment and we'll take a look.

Thanks for looking out for each other.
