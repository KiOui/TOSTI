How to verify your age with Yivi?

After verification you will be able to open the beer fridge!

T.O.S.T.I. uses Yivi for verifying your age online. Yivi was created on the Radboud University and is a system that allows you to only share the data that is absolutely necessary for a third party. In this case, T.O.S.T.I. will not get to know your birthdate but rather only whether you are over 18 years old or not. More information about Yivi can be found on their website.


1. Install the Yivi app

If you want to verify your age with Yivi, you need to download the Yivi app on the App Store or Google Play Store. After that, you can set up an account in the app.


2. Scan the QR code

After your account is set up, you can click the button on the age verification page. A popup will come up and show the Yivi QR code. Open the QR code scanner in the Yivi app and scan the QR code.


3. Load the required attributes

The Yivi app will now show the information that T.O.S.T.I. needs to verify. You can load this information by following the steps in the app. Currently, T.O.S.T.I. requires two pieces of information:

  1. Whether you are older than 18 years: This attribute can be loaded using multiple methods such as via DigID or your municipality.
  2. Your student number: This attribute can be loaded using the SURF login and is used to verify that your T.O.S.T.I. account corresponds to the account in Yivi.

Make sure to log in with the same student number as the one with which you are logged in to T.O.S.T.I. If you use a different student number the age verification will not work.


4. Share the attributes with T.O.S.T.I.

Once you loaded the required attributes, you can share them with T.O.S.T.I. by following the steps in the Yivi app. T.O.S.T.I. will give you a success message and the page will reload, after the page reload your age will be verified.
