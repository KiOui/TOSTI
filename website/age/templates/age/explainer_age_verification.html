Verify your age with Yivi

A one-time setup so the beer fridge will open for you.

Verification runs through Yivi — a privacy-friendly identity wallet built at Radboud University. The clever bit: it lets you share just the answer to a specific question without giving up the underlying data. Here, the question is "is this person over 18?" — TOSTI never sees your actual birthdate. There's more on the Yivi website if you're curious.


1. Install the Yivi app

Download it on your phone — App Store, Google Play, or F-Droid.


2. Scan the QR code

Open your age verification page, click , and scan the QR code with the Yivi app on your phone.


3. Load the required attributes

The app will tell you what TOSTI is asking for and walk you through loading it from the source. Two things:

  1. Proof you're over 18. The Yivi app accepts this from DigID, iDIN, or your municipality — pick whichever works for you.
  2. Your student number, provided through SURF. This is how TOSTI knows the Yivi credential belongs to the same person as your TOSTI account — the two student numbers have to match or verification fails.


4. Share with TOSTI

Once the attributes are loaded, the Yivi app prompts you to share them with TOSTI. Confirm in the app and the page reloads — you're verified. The fridge will open for you during opening hours from now on.
