Age check

Some actions (such as opening the beer fridge) require you to proof that you are 18 years or older. To proof that you are of a certain age, you can do two things:

  1. Ask a staff member to verify your birthdate by using a passport or identity card.
  2. Use the online verification method below to proof that you have a certain age.

The first method does not require much explanation but the second method requires some more setup. If you want more information about using the online verification, please read the explainer on the Explainers page.
