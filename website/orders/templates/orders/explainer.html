How to order products?

Before you can start ordering products on TOSTI, first login using your Science account via the login page. Then, go to this page to see if there is an active shift available where you can order. If an active shift is available, the page will look like the following.

Click on a venue you want to order at. You will see the following screen.

Click on "Order items", the button will take you to the order page where you can select products. Note that you can not order unlimited products, there are restrictions on your maximum order and on the amount of orders per item. The page will display when you have ordered the maximum amount in total and the maximum amount of an item. When you have added enough items to your cart, click on the "Order items" button to order the items. You can always order more items if you are not at your maximum yet.

After ordering items, your order page will look like this, displaying your orders (and other orders if more people placed orders) with the status of your order. Orders with a icon are your orders, orders without a icon are orders from other users.

You may have noticed that your orders are displaying a Not paid button. This button can have four separate values:

  • Not paid : You did not pay your order yet and your order is not finished.
  • Processing : You have paid your order and it is still processing.
  • Done : You have not paid your order and it is finished.
  • Done : You have paid your order and it is finished.

Orders from other people can have two states.

  • Processing : The order is still being made.
  • Done : The order is ready.

When your order is ready, you can pick it up at the venue you ordered. Have fun ordering!
