Process a deposit transaction

For bakers handling cans at the counter.

Some canteen items have a deposit on them. When a customer brings the empties back, you credit their TOSTI balance — or, if they want to spend balance, you subtract from it. Here's the flow.


1. Be in a running shift

Deposit transactions only work from the baker view of an active shift. If you haven't started one, see the "Manage a shift" tab above.


2. Make sure the customer has a TOSTI account

The customer needs a TOSTI account to hold the balance. They open one from their Transactions tab — one-time setup. The "Hand in deposit" tab walks them through it if they're new.


3. Ask for their QR code

They open their personal QR code from the button in the top-right corner of any TOSTI page.


4. Scan it

On the shift admin page, hit the scanner button in the footer. Accept the camera permission, point at their QR code, and the scanner pulls up their account.


5. Add the transaction

A popup shows the customer's name and current balance. Add a positive amount to credit them (handing in deposit) or a negative amount to debit them (spending balance). The new balance updates immediately.

If the customer is also paying for an order in the same visit, you can apply a discount manually after recording the transaction.
