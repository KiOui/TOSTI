How to hand in deposit or use your balance?

Get a discount by handing in deposit!

The canteens sell items that sometimes have a deposit on them (for example, the tin cans with soda). These cans can be handed in at the counter for recycling. If you hand in a used can with deposit, you will receive a balance in T.O.S.T.I. that can be used for discount on future purchases. This guide will show you how to open an account and hand in tin cans to get a discount on your purchases!


1. Open an account

Before you can hand in a deposit, you need to open an account in T.O.S.T.I. You can open an account by going to the "Transactions" tab under the Account view.


2. Go to the counter

You can now go to the counter to either hand in some deposit or use your balance. Tell the person behind the counter what you would like to do.


3. Open your QR code

The person behind the counter can only access your account with a valid QR code. You can open your QR code by clicking the button in the header.

The person behind the counter will now scan your QR code and process your deposit or discount!
