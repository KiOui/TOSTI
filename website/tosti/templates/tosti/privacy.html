Privacy

About the data we collect

Who are we

This website is being maintained by Tartarus, the foundation managing the student canteens of the Faculty of Science of the Radboud University Nijmegen. We can be reached via tartarus@science.ru.nl, or for technical matters, via www-tartarus@science.ru.nl

Your account

To use most features of this site, you need a user account. The accounts are provided via the Faculty of Science's OpenID service. When you log in for the first time, your username, full name and email address are requested via this OpenID server and stored.

When you use your account for services, such as ordering products or requesting songs, we naturally store this data too.

The data is stored on the grounds of legitimate interest (when you use the music-control feature) and contractual necessity (for ordering products). Your name will be visible to other users when you order a product or when you request a song. Your email address will never be shown to other users.

How long we keep your data

Your account will be kept for a period of a year after your last login and will, without notification, be removed after you haven't used it for more than a year. However, whenever your credentials for the Faculty of Science OpenID service expire, you won't be able to access your account anymore either.

Your song requests and orders will be stored for a year and will be anonymized afterwards.

If you want to access your data

If you want to access your the data we have of you, you can contact us via email and we will provide you with the records we keep. This holds as well for if you want your data to be deleted or rectified.

Google Analytics

In addition to the data above, this website also uses Google Analytics for generating visitor statistics. However, this data is collected anonymously and will also not be linked to the account of logged in users.

Data Protection Authority

In case you think we have processed your data unlawfully and wish to file a complaint, you always have the right to do so at the Dutch Data Protection Authority, the Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens.
